## Engagement Rules

1. **Join groups 48 hours before posting** - Don't spam immediately
2. **Engage with 5-10 other posts first** - Build presence
3. **No direct sales links in posts** - Use "DM me" or "check my profile"
4. **Reply to EVERY comment** - Build relationships
5. **Post 1x per group per week MAX** - Don't get banned
6. **Add value first** - 80% value, 20% pitch
7. **Use questions** - Higher engagement than statements
